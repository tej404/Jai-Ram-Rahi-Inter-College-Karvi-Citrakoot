About the School

Jai Ram Rahi Inter College, established in 1978, is a well-recognized co-educational institution in Karwi, Chitrakoot. The school is affiliated with the Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UP Board) and offers classes from 1st to 12th.

Our goal is to provide holistic education that not only focuses on academics but also emphasizes character building, moral values, and life skills. We believe in nurturing young minds and shaping responsible citizens for the future.

The school is known for its disciplined environment, experienced faculty, and a culture of excellence. We welcome all students and parents to become a part of our educational journey.
